Privacy & Support - CourtClarity

Effective Date: September 25, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you use CourtClarity, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Your email address or username if you create an account.
  • Usage Data: Interactions with the app, features accessed, device type, and other analytics.
  • Location Data: Optional city-level data to provide relevant resources in Louisiana.

2. How We Collect Information

We collect information directly when you use the app, interact with the CourtClarity assistant, or provide optional data like your location.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide and improve the app experience.
  • Personalize resources and suggestions relevant to your legal queries.
  • Maintain and manage your account.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

You can request deletion of your data or account at any time. CourtClarity will remove all personally identifiable information upon request.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to manage, access, and delete your data at any time. You may also withdraw consent for data collection where applicable.
